About me

I'm Victoria (Tori) and I started running with high school cross country in Fall 2000. I didn't have a lot of success with 5Ks, and in college decided to try out longer distances. After a tough trail half marathon that took me 3.5 hours, I figured 26.2 miles on roads couldn't be much worse. I ran my first marathon in 2005, and quickly got hooked.

In need of a goal, I set my sights on running a Boston-qualifying time of 3:40 (at the time), but that was proving to be difficult. While scouring the internet for marathon tips, I came across the 50 States Marathon Club, and decided running a marathon in every state would be a fun way to stay active and goal-oriented while seeing the country.

I try to be cost-effecient while traveling, and am a road warrior and adventure enthusiast. I've driven over 800 miles to get to a marathon, and book flights with flyer miles whenever possible. My plan is to run as many marathons as I can under 4 hours, but also to seek out races in interesting and beautiful places.
